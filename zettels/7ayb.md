Tips from the fantastic blog of Dr. Yorgey[^yorgey].

1. Keep a calendar
1. Take notes
1. Set aside a study space ([[u3ia|Spaceship You]])
1. Minimize distractions
1. Use pomodoros
1. Schedule (discipline == freedom)
1. Be consistent
1. Be intentional and proactive (communication)
1. Find a mentor
1. Find more mentors
